About BerningStudios

BerningStudios is a modern, artist-focused recording space built around a hybrid analog/digital workflow. Whether you're cutting your first single or finishing a full-length album, the mission remains the same: capture honest performances and deliver mixes that feel powerful, clean, and release-ready.

From songwriting and pre-production to final masters, you’ll get a relaxed, collaborative environment with the technical backbone to compete on today’s streaming platforms.

What you can expect
  • Clear communication about timelines, budget, and deliverables
  • Session prep help (tempo maps, reference tracks, track planning)
  • A space designed for creativity and confidence — not pressure

Meet AJ Berning

AJ Berning is the engineer and producer behind BerningStudios — helping artists translate ideas into finished records that feel honest, loud, and ready for release.

  • Specialties: indie, rock, pop, and worship
  • Services: recording, mixing, mastering & production coaching
  • Approach: relaxed vibe, clear feedback, collaborative team energy
